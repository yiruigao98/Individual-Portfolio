The Stories of Three Teams and I

These are my three favorite teams: Chelsea football club from England, the Golden States Warriors and the Guangdong Southeast Tigers from China Basketball Association.
There is a long story between me and these three teams. I started playing basketball when I was very young, in the primary school. At that time, I started to watch CBA games and became a fans with Guangdong Southeast Tigers team since then.
After that, I watched soccer games and was admired with Drogba's wonderful skills. I came to be a Warrior fans after Klay Thompson entered the league. I liked his three-point shooting, as well as the spirit of never saying no. Curry and him became a pair of good partners for shooters for nearly ten years. And I will stand with Klay as he is currently my idol in NBA.
